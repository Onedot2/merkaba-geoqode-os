🥽 Quest Quickstart

Get into VR in 3 steps — no app install

Works on Meta Quest 2, Quest 3, and Quest Pro. Uses your built-in browser. Nothing to download.

1
Put on your Quest headset Press the Quest button on your controller to open the home environment.
2
Open Meta Quest Browser Find the Browser app in your App Library, or say "Hey Meta, open browser." Navigate to realaios.com/vr
3
Pick an experience & tap Enter VR Mode Choose any of the 20 live experiences from the grid. Tap the big 🥽 Enter VR Mode button. You're in.
✦
Tip: bookmark realaios.com/vr In the Quest Browser, tap the star icon to bookmark. Next time: one tap to enter.

Not on Quest? AIOS VR works on any WebXR-capable browser — Chrome on PC, Edge, or Firefox Reality. On desktop, click Enter VR Mode and it'll use your PC VR headset if connected.

Common Questions

Things people ask first

Is this actually free to try?
All 20 VR experiences are completely free — no account, no credit card. The AI assistant (STORMchat) has a free tier (10 messages/day) and paid plans from $12/mo. Developer tools are free at the OS layer.
Do I need to install anything for VR?
No. AIOS VR uses WebXR — a web standard built into the Meta Quest Browser. Just open realaios.com/vr on your headset. No app download, no sideloading, no accounts required.
What's the Merkaba lattice? What does 8→26→48:480 mean?
It's the geometric architecture AIOS uses for all decision-routing, memory, and service discovery. Think of it as the "address system" inside the OS — each service lives at a node in a 48-node lattice, and decisions flow through it in a verified pattern. You don't need to understand it to use AIOS. If you want the deep dive, check /ai.
What does "dual-verified AI decisions" mean?
Every AI output goes through two independent AI agents scanning from opposite geometric poles (PHI=1.618 and PSI=1.414). Both must agree before the answer ships. This means hallucinations and contradictions get caught before they reach you — not after.
How is AIOS different from ChatGPT / Copilot / Claude?
Those are AI models. AIOS is an operating system layer that sits above models. It decides which model to use, verifies the output with a second agent, routes the result to the right service, and self-heals if anything fails. You can use any model (including GPT-4o) inside AIOS — you're buying the layer that makes models trustworthy and production-safe.
Who built this?
AIOS is built by Brains4Ai — a private AI studio. The founder is Bradley Levitan. The full system spec is auditable at realaios.com/ai. VR platform, AI assistant, and developer tools are all live and running 24/7.
